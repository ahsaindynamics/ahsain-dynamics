Matasa Innovators

From teaching
to building
innovators.

Matasa Innovators began as Tech Talent Hub — a small group of 10 students learning Arduino and embedded systems in Kano. It grew into something far bigger.

The mission evolved: not just to teach skills, but to build problem-solvers, creators, and entrepreneurs who use technology to create real-world impact across Nigeria and beyond.

"Matasa" means Youth in Hausa — because this is built for Northern Nigeria's next generation of builders.
